Nut Biscuit Recipe

Ingredients

  • - 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • - 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • - 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • - 1/2 cup finely chopped nuts (e.g., almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • - A pinch of salt

Baking Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
    Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
    Add vanilla extract and mix. Sift in the flour, add a pinch of salt, and fold in finely chopped nuts.
  3. Form the dough into small balls or use a cookie scoop to drop portions onto the prepared baking sheet.
    Leave space between each ball for spreading.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the edges turn golden brown.
  5. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  6. Once cooled, your Nut Biscuits are ready to be enjoyed!
    Pair them with your favorite beverage and savor the delightful nutty flavor.
